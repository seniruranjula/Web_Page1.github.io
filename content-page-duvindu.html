ORIGINE

Indoor games were originally invented to help people amuse themselves within the confines of their homes. They were invented to pass time at home when, either the weather was bad or, when, sickness confined a person to his or her bed. Indoor games also meant, mothers could keep a watchful eye on their children while doing their housework.With urbanisation indoor games are becoming more popular. In capital and metro cities a few schools will have their own playgrounds. It is because these these cities and centres have hardly any open space for outdoor games. So more and more institutions are encouraging indoor games.


Overview

Indoor games means game that are played,situated,done or used inside a building for example an indoor swimming pool championship, indoor 200metres championship. It usually means any kind of games or sports organised in the building or inside the room are indoor games.

There are various, actually enormous indoor games which children can play. Even the parents can also play alongwith their children. Some of the them are,

  • Chess,Table tennis,Badmintain,Ludo,Basketball,Indoor Bowling,Indoor Cricket,Futsel

    • Benifits of Indoor Sports

    For kids, it is crucial to playing freely to relax and have some fun; instead, a majority of kids today tend to spend most of their time at home just watching television or playing video games. Indoor play areas and indoor games have proven to be a great outlet for kids. This is because they can be active, have fun, and most importantly perhaps play in a clean, safe, and colorful environment. Most well designed and modern indoor play areas feature slides, tunnels, ball pools, bridges, adventure zones, trampolines, and other equipment which can keep your kids engaged for several hours. Regularly playing indoor games such as ping pong helps your kids improve their body fitness since these games incorporate a variety of body movements. Also, experts state that regularly engaging in indoor games such as table tennis helps in developing fast-twitch muscles which can ultimately improve your kid’s reflexes.During the time spent in indoor games, kids usually face several situations and challenges which help them in developing vital skills. For example, interacting with others can help them develop and improve their imagination.
